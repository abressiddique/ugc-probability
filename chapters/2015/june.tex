and $Y\sim poisson(\lambda)$for some $\lambda > 0$.Suppose E[X]=E[Y].Then
\begin{enumerate}
    \item $var(X)=Var(Y)$\\
    \item $var(X)<Var(Y)$\\
    \item $var(Y)<Var(X)$\\
    \item $Var(X)$ may be larger or smaller than $Var(Y)$ depending on the values of n,p and $\lambda$
\end{enumerate}
